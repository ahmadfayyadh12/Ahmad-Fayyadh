Most recent works

New Student Registration Information and Management System

The New Student Registration Information and Management System is a digital solution that simplifies the student admission process in educational institutions. With features such as online registration, digital document management, automatic verification systems, and capacity management, this application helps institutions efficiently manage admissions. Automatic notifications and tracking systems facilitate communication between the institution and prospective students, while strong data security ensures the protection of sensitive information. Thus, this application not only enhances administrative effectiveness but also provides a more transparent and convenient registration experience for all parties involved.

e-Presensi Tanjung Jabung Timur

The e-Presensi Tanjung Jabung Timur is an advanced web-based attendance solution for civil servants. Managed through a web system, this application supports attendance input through Fingerspot machines or Android devices. With 4,042 active users, this system has been the backbone of daily attendance recording since 2022. Its reliability makes it an essential tool in attendance management, providing high efficiency and accuracy for users in East Tanjung Jabung Regency. (Development Team)

Demo

Android e-Presensi Tanjung Jabung Timur

The Android version of the e-Presensi Tanjung Jabung Timur Application offers quick and efficient attendance input. Users can easily view summaries of their personal attendance. Security is reinforced by the requirement to be within a radius of their respective office premises, ensuring location and attendance accuracy. This feature not only provides convenience in attendance recording but also enhances data security and integrity. With a user-friendly interface, this Android application provides a smooth experience to meet the needs of civil servants in East Tanjung Jabung Regency. (Development Team)

Brain Tumor Detection Using Modification Lu-Net Based on MRI Images

A Python-based brain tumor detection application using a modified Lu-Net algorithm provides high accuracy in lesion identification and segmentation. With an intuitive interface, users can upload medical images, and the application quickly analyzes them to detect potential brain tumors. Customized Lu-Net technology ensures precise detection and accurate mapping, supporting further steps in diagnosis and treatment planning. This innovative solution is not only efficient in early detection but also significantly contributes to the development of technology in the healthcare field.
